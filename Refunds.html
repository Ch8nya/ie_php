Refunds

At InternEase, we strive to deliver high-quality programs and services that meet your expectations. However, we understand that circumstances may arise where you may be dissatisfied with our offerings or unable to continue participating. In such cases, we have established a fair and transparent refund policy to protect your interests.

Program Refunds

If we fail to assign you a paid internship withing 7 days after the end of the program, you are entitled to a full refund.

Internship Stipend Refunds

During your internship experience with InternEase, you will receive a stipend as per our Internship Terms and Conditions. However, if you choose to terminate your internship prematurely, you may be required to refund a portion of the stipend received, calculated based on the duration of your internship. Participants will receive their stipend on a weekly cadence, calculated based on a predefined monthly compensation scale.

Refund Process

To initiate a refund request, please submit your request in writing to nidhisengupta@tutamail.com, providing detailed reasons for your request and any supporting documentation. Our team will review your request and respond within 10 business days.

If your refund request is approved, the refund amount will be processed within 30 business days and credited back to the original payment method used for enrollment or stipend payment.

Exceptions and Limitations

Please note that refunds may not be applicable in certain circumstances, such as:

- Non-compliance with program or internship
- Misconduct or violation of terms and conditions

InternEase reserves the right to deny refund requests in cases of abuse, fraud, or any other breach of our policies or applicable laws.

By participating in our programs or internships, you acknowledge and agree to this refund policy. If you have any further questions or concerns, please contact our support team for assistance.
